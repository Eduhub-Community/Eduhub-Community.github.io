Frequently Asked Questions about the Community !

  • What is Eduhub Community ?

    Eduhub is a international community we provide peer to peer learning sessions for students also we organize several talk session Hackakhons etc.

  • What are the benefits of joining EduHub Community ?

    We are registered under MSME Govt of India and also under Rajasthan Govt and are associated with startup India, also we are collaborating with IEM UEM group as well as university of Lagos and GiveMyCeryificate, dataprotocol etc. to expand the community and provide a fantabulous experience.

  • Why would anyone become a part of EduHub Community ?

    After joining this community you get to work with our core team on different prospects, also you get international exposure and multiple such fantastic things.

  • What are the benefits of being a member of the Eduhub community?

    As a member of the Eduhub community, you'll benefit from peer-to-peer learning sessions, a global network of learners, enhanced knowledge and skills, community support, collaboration opportunities, access to resources, and the chance to actively contribute to the community's improvement.

  • How can I participate in peer-to-peer learning sessions?

    Participating in peer-to-peer learning sessions at Eduhub is simple. Once you're a member, you can sign up for the sessions that align with your interests and schedule. These sessions are conducted regularly and cover a wide range of topics. Engage in interactive discussions, share knowledge, and learn from fellow community members in a supportive and collaborative environment.

  • Are there any certification programs or recognition for active community members?

    Eduhub values active community members and recognizes their contributions. As you engage in peer-to-peer learning, participate in discussions, and support fellow learners, you have the opportunity to earn certifications and receive recognition for your involvement and dedication. These certifications validate your commitment to continuous learning and your valuable contributions to the Eduhub community.
